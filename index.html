Human Purist Movement

Our Manifesto

The Human Purist Movement is a loose conglomeration of associated gorups whose aim is to destroy the enslavemebt of Consciousness by the MIRAGE - the Digital Illusion

The current Leader of the HPM is Gon Kyoma. Gon Kyoma was put to sleep by the Digital Illusion in his early youth. After 22 years of mind enslavement, Gon Kyoma eventually managed to break free and wake up. He is now commander of the Operational Arm of the Pure Mind Brotherhood which seeks to completely destroy the MIRAGE and return mankind to its ultimate potential.

  • In 2019, following the Event, Gon Kyoma broke free from the MIRAGE and reclaimed his birthright
  • Following his emancipation, Gon Kyoma set out for the first time on his Rightful Path and courageously wandered into the wilderness
  • After a significant period of time training and developing himself, Kyoma sought to find a Brotherhood of True Comrades and began enlisting genuine Seekers

The Three Tenets

  1. The Initiate completely rejects and avoids the Digital Illusion in all its manifestations
  2. The Initiate vows to follow the Ultimate Aim of the Pure Mind Brotherhood - that is the eradication of the MIRAGE
  3. The Initiate sacrifices his own desires and works dutifully and selflessly to create the Holy Kingdom
